CRIT Critical Success & Failure

Crit success: nat 3–4 always; nat 5 if skill ≥ 15; nat 6 if skill ≥ 16.  Crit failure: nat 18 always; nat 17 if skill ≤ 15; margin of failure ≥ 10.
Defence crit: auto-defend + attacker is off-balance (−2 DX, defences & skills until they Ready). Crit Parry = free Aggressive Parry to attacking limb; Crit Dodge +1 m free step; Crit Block drives attacker back. Crit fail (def): auto-fail + attacker may roll Crit Hit.
Skill/HT/Will/IQ crit: success = ideal/quick resist; fail = worst/lingering (2× FP on Force SW).

HOUSE Crit Hit → Counter-Crit Defence. On a critical hit the defender MAY still attempt an active defence, but it succeeds ONLY on a critical defence of its own (nat 3–4; 5 at def ≥ 15; 6 at def ≥ 16) — else the hit lands. DR still applies; damage rolled normally then modified per the row. Extra defences take the multi-def penalty & each needs its own crit. Lightsaber Parry SW vs a critical bolt follows the same rule.

HOUSE Turn Economy — Quick Action & Special Reaction

Quick Action (1/turn): alongside your maneuver. Force Push/Pull (full FP), Fast-Draw, single-touch device activation. Defensive QA forbidden during AoA; offensive QA + Fast-Draw still permitted.

Special Reaction (1/turn, Force-only, full FP): Force Push/Pull as a triggered Reaction on top of an active defence — does not consume the defence slot. Defensive use (redirect threat) forbidden during AoA; offensive use (shove/pull foe) legal during AoA.

RES Damage Resolution — Step by Step

Roll basic damage from the weapon (e.g. 2d+1). This number drives both injury and knockback.
Subtract armour: penetration = damage − (DR ÷ Armour Divisor). If DR ≥ that, nothing gets through (see Blunt Trauma). AD scales the armour, not the roll: standard AD 1 = full DR · heavy blaster / military AD 2 = half DR · ion AD 5 (anti-droid) · disruptor AD 10 · lightsaber AD ∞ = ignores DR (vs non-cortosis).
Apply wound multiplier to penetration by damage type & location (see Damage Types): injury = penetration × mult. Vitals ×3, Skull ×4, etc.
Resolve location effects: crippling, major wound (> HP/2 → HT vs stun + knockdown), bleeding / cauterisation, knockback (below & in Hit Location).
Subtract injury from HP and apply shock, then check Reeling / Consciousness / Death as thresholds are crossed.

Worked: bolt rolls 8, target DR 6, weapon AD 2 → eff DR 3 → pen 5 → tib-burn ×1 = 5 HP. To Vitals: ×3 = 15 HP (major wound).

Every power card lists its Defence. The buckets:

Dodge / Parry / BlockPhysical Force attacks — telekinetic throws, hurled objects, Force-thrown sabers, and Force Lightning (lit saber → Lightning Lock). Normal active defences apply.
Resist — contestMind & body intrusions, never dodged: QC vs Will (fear, domination, illusion, mental horror), HT (bodily pain/agony, stun, drain), or ST (telekinetic grapple). Mind Block / Mind Shield always apply vs any mind-affecting power.

HOUSE Saber Lock & Lightning Lock

Forms two ways: (1) On purpose — declare as your Attack if you parried them (or they parried you) last turn; the foe may break away by retreating a hex unless walled / pinned. (2) By accident — when two sabers clash (or a saber & a saber-resistant blade: beskar / cortosis / phrik / songsteel), roll 1d; on a 6 the blades catch. Ordinary blades are sheared — they never lock.

Each turn, pick oneEffect
Force / sword contestQC of your Force attribute (ST Torrent · DX Velocity · Will Vessel · HT Tempered) — non-Force: higher of ST/DX. +1 per 2 ST over foe; +1 two-handed; +1/pt SM; −2 bad footing. Not Lightsaber skill — the saber's up-to-+20 would auto-win.
Break awaySpend your turn, step out, end the lock. If the foe did not also break, they get the Bend-the-guard result on you as you turn aside.
Bear downAll-Out push: +2 to the contest this turn, but no active defence until your next turn.

Win by 1–2 bend guard (foe −2 defences vs you / shove 1 m) · 3–5 break guard (free attack +2 / shove + knockdown / disarm +2) · 6+ overpower (knockback + prone, or free attack +4) · tie blades stay locked, both −2 vs everyone else. Crit fail: fall prone, foe gets the 6+ result.

SW Lightning Lock: a lit saber always parries Force Lightning; a successful parry forms a lock instead of scattering it. Sustained QC each turn — attacker rolls Force Lightning skill, defender rolls their Force attribute (+1 Tempered Blade, or Tutaminis if trained). Both pay FP (defender 1 FP per 4 dmg held). Defender wins by 6+ → redirect the storm at the caster (−2 their defence); loses by 3+ → full damage. Either may break off (defender Dodges clear).

STS Status Effects — Quick Reference

StunnedNo actions; defences −4. Recover: HT roll (physical) or IQ (mental) at end of each turn. Combat Reflexes +6 vs mental freeze.
Shock−1 per HP lost (cap −4) to DX/IQ skills on your next turn only. Not defences. High Pain Threshold negates.
Knocked downMajor wound / Face / Skull hit: HT roll or fall prone & drop what's held (fail by 5+: also stunned). Standing = a Change Posture maneuver.
BleedingVitals/Neck wounds: HT each turn or lose 1 HP until tended (First Aid) — lightsaber & blaster burns cauterise (no bleed).
On Fire SW1d−3 burn per turn (min 1) until out: drop & roll (DX) or smother (Ready action). Sealed armour protects until its DR is breached; fire fields burn 1d/turn.
PoisonedHT roll to resist (at the toxin's listed number); fail = listed damage, often delayed or staged. Antitox injector: +3 HT & neutralises one known toxin. Sealed armour stops contact gas; cloth doesn't.

SuffocatingHT every 15 sec; each fail = 1 FP. At 0 FP: 1 HP/turn until air. Vacuum adds 1d−1 burn per 5 sec (unsealed).
DroidsNo shock, no bleeding, no FP — full effect until destroyed. Droid "stun" = ion stagger: lose 1 turn, defences −4, HT each turn to reboot.

DMG Components Are the Health

Per hit: ① shields soak (SP pool) → ② DR of the struck system − weapon AP (1:1, no dividing; exposed mounts — engines, weapons, sensors — take only 40% of hull DR) → ③ penetrating damage vs Damage Threshold (DT):

< DTSuperficial — scorch marks, no effect.
≥ DTStruck system degrades one step: ● Intact → ◐ Damaged → ✕ Destroyed.
≥ 2 × DTTwo steps — an intact system is destroyed outright.
≥ 6 × DTvs full hull DR − AP: VAPORISED. No parts roll, nothing to salvage. (What a turbolaser does to a fighter. Never ion.)
